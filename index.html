My Journey

I'm a student developer with a strong passion for creating beautiful, functional, and user-centered digital experiences. My journey into programming began during my college years, where I quickly discovered my love for problem-solving and logical thinking through code. Since then, I have continuously worked on improving my skills by building web applications and exploring modern web technologies to create clean, responsive, and seamless user experiences.


Beyond classroom learning, I actively participate in coding challenges, and programming competitions to sharpen my technical skills and stay updated with current industry trends. I am proud to have won 1st Place in the Programming Skills Competition during the CCSIT Day event at our college, as well as achieving 2nd Place in the Easter Visayas Coding Olympics (EVCO) – Java Category. I have also represented my school as a participant in the National CodeChum Competition, which further strengthened my problem-solving abilities, teamwork, and resilience under pressure.


These experiences have helped me develop a strong foundation in programming, collaboration, and continuous learning. I am highly motivated to grow as a developer, take on real-world challenges, and contribute my skills to meaningful projects while continuously improving both my technical and creative abilities.
